Questions

How does billing work?

You pay once and it's active for 30 days. After that it returns to the free tier — no automatic charges. You choose when to renew.

Can I still use my own API keys on a paid plan?

Yes. Your own API keys always work on any plan. The paid AI Pro plan just also gives you access to all models using Lunet's site keys.

How does payment reach you?

Payments go through Stripe and are deposited directly to your connected bank account or debit card.

What if I want to cancel?

Since it's not an auto-renewing subscription, you just don't repurchase when your month ends. Your plan downgrades to free automatically.
